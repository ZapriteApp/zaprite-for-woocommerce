Zaprite Payment Gateway is a WooCommerce payment gateway that allows you to accept Bitcoin, Lightning, Liquid and card payments.

== Description ==

Zaprite Payment Gateway for WooCommerce enables your ecommerce store to accept Bitcoin, Lightning, Liquid and card payments in one unified Checkout experience.
This plugin integrates seamlessly with your WooCommerce Checkout, providing a fast, secure, and low-cost payment option for your customers.

**Features:**
- Accept Bitcoin, Lightning and Liquid payments directly to your wallet.
- Accept card payments via Stripe or Square.
- Easy setup and configuration within WooCommerce settings.
- QR code generation for easy payments.
- Transaction history and CSV export for easy reconciliation.

**Why Choose Zaprite?**
- Lower transaction fees compared to traditional payment methods.
- Enhanced security and privacy for your customers.
- No intermediaries or third parties, receive payments directly.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `zaprite-payment-gateway` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to WooCommerce > Settings > Payments.
4. Click on 'Zaprite' to configure the payment gateway settings.
